This is your practice exam.

How to take the exam:
(a)
Print out the PDF of the exam (or work on a separate sheet of paper).
(b)
Take the exam for – minutes. If it is easy and you have solved all the problems, you may finish early. If you are stuck, try to fight-it-out for the full time.
(c)
Check your answers online with Ximera. The Ximera version of the practice exam will be posted online sometime after the PDF is posted.

When you come to your exam you will need to know:

(a)
Your name.
(b)
Your lastname.number.
(c)
Your instructor’s name.
(d)
Your recitation leader’s name.
(e)
Your recitation time.

One point will be taken off for each missing piece of information.

The real exam will have the following written on it:

(a)
Do not look inside the exam until instructed to do so.
(b)
Write your name above.
(c)
You have minutes to complete the exam.
(d)
Calculators or cell phones are prohibited.
(e)
Justify your answers for full credit.
(f)
Show your work for partial credit.
(g)
Write your answers on the included pages.
(h)
Provide answers for all questions asked.

The real exam is not limited to question from the practice exam. Any topic from the course is fair-game on an in-class examination. However, at least 50% of the real exam will be very similar to the practice exam.
